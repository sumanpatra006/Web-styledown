About Us

The ACM-XIM Chapter is a student-led organization at XIM University, fostering creativity and innovation through technology. Our mission is to empower students with skills in web development, artificial intelligence, IoT, and more.

Through events, workshops, and hackathons, we provide hands-on learning experiences and a platform for like-minded individuals to network and grow.

Upcoming Events

  • Web Styledown Challenge: A 48-hour web design hackathon to showcase your creative skills.
  • Tech Talks: Dive into trending topics like AI ethics and IoT innovations with industry experts.
  • Hands-on Workshops: Learn web development, machine learning, and more from scratch!
